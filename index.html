Channels is a Processing library that lets you extract the red, green, blue, brightness, hue, or saturation channels from a PImage. It works similarly to Processing's built-in red(), green(), blue(), brightness(), hue(), and saturation() functions, but instead of applying to individual pixels, Channels' equivalent functions apply to the entire image.
